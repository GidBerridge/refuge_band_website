REFUGE was formed a few years ago when a Welsh singer and a Chinese lead guitarist met in a small room in a Camden pub and hit it off immediately.
The pair started to write music and as the character of the band took shape ...
they added a Nigerian bass player ...
an Italian keyboardist ...
and finally an English drummer ...
which completed the multi-national REFUGE line-up ...
Musically REFUGE have formed a unique amalgamation of indie and rock ...
and merging elements of funk, pop, and blues into their music ...
Most importantly REFUGE combine captivating melodies and meaningful lyrics into their music ...
producing catchy and crowd-pleasing tracks that leave you wanting more!
