REFUGE was formed a few years ago when a Welsh singer and a Chinese lead guitarist met in a small room in a Camden pub and hit it off immediately.
The pair started to write music and as the character of the band took shape ...
they added a Nigerian bass player ...
an Italian keyboardist ...
and finally an English drummer ...
which completed the multi-national REFUGE line-up ...
Musically REFUGE have formed a unique amalgamation of indie and rock ...
and merging elements of funk, pop, and blues into their music ...
Most importantly REFUGE combine captivating melodies and meaningful lyrics into their music ...
producing catchy and crowd-pleasing tracks that leave you wanting more!

Joe

The boss, our frontman, songwriter, the booming voice, and the driving force behind the band. He's never happier than when he is on stage performing.

Kun

The enigma that is our awesome lead guitarist, does he eat, does he sleep, does he talk? Well he lets his guitar do the talking and he's hands down one of the best guitarists in the uk right now.

Ike

Mr Funky himself, there is not a song this man cannot play, literally a bass master. Although he'd never say himself as hes such a humble and cool person, Ike has mastered many styles of bass playing with hard work and practice...in fact it's hard to stop him playing and turning his bass amp to the max.

Matteo

Our lovely Italian keys player and king of the flowery shirts adds that bit of texture to our sound aswell as backing vocals. Just make sure that he is fed at regular 2 hour intervals and Matteo is a happy man.

Louis

The steady heartbeat of the band, big Lou makes sure we are all in time and keeps us all in check as well as being a multi instrumentalist. They say that all drummers are crazy, and Louis is absolutely no exception to this, but in a good way so whatever state he turns up in he always plays on time, everytime.
